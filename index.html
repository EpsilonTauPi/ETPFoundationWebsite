About Us

Epsilon Tau Pi is a fraternal organization of past recipients of the Eagle Scout Award given by the Boy Scouts of America. For further information about the national fraternity click here.


The Epsilon Tau foundation was created for the charitable and educational purpose of collecting donations and awarding partial educational scholarships to individuals who have received the Eagle Scout Award. These scholarships will be made to help pay the cost of the recipient’s pursuit of a Bachelor’s or Associate’s Degree. These scholarships shall be paid directly to the educational institution attended by the recipients, for the recipients benefit. Scholarships shall be awarded to first year students who are attending educational institutions that maintain active chapters of the Epsilon Tau Pi Fraternity. Funding for the Applicant’s activities shall be through contributions from the general public and through other permitted fundraising efforts.
